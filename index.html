Unplanned Backlog is a list of all issues raised by clients to get addressed by Adenza for seamless functioning of the solution offered (Calypso & AxioSL).
This backlog is prioritized and actioned based upon Client impact assessment (Escalation, Business Impact, Delivery Required, Commercial Impact) which is determined by a number of teams including Product Support, CSM, CD.
Delivery teams review and deliver fixes basis priortisation (Scoring) and communicate the delivery plan by ensuring that Status, FixVersion and Target Release date are communicated throughout the process on a regular cadence.
